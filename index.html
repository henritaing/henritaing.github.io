Relationship mapping - A way to map your friends for your parents to remember

I am lucky to have made many friends during the different periods of my life.

But it also means that when I tell my mom about them, she keeps forgetting who's who and how I got to know them.

So, as a mean to draw her a clearer picture, I had this idea of mapping my friends like we'd map processes in consulting (-> #youcanleaveconsultingbutconsultingdoesn'tleaveyou).

Then I realized that I could use it to see who could fit with whom and it comes into handy since I love making crossovers between my different friends' groups, albeit not too successful sometimes.

Feel free to try yourself!
